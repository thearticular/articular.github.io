Homepage

Vocals, instrumentations and images were generated by various local generative models like Riffusion, Stable Diffusion, Bark, Chirp, MusicGen, Flux. Mixing and sampling in FL Studio. Audio refinements in Audition. Visuals in Photoshop. Animations and motion graphics in After Effects. Video editing in Premiere Pro. Downscaled with Handbrake. And UI/UX with JavasScript and Web Audio API. Most of the lyrics are from various ancient literary sources from around the world, mostly focused on the dead languages from before 1000 BCE. As I love history and music, I have tried bringing the past alive.
